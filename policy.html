Policy & FAQ

Everything you need to know about our services

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take for my photos to be delivered? ▼
Our standard photo editing and delivery time is 5–7 business days. We're often able to deliver ahead of schedule. Our commitment is to provide high-quality results in the shortest possible time.
Do you edit the pictures? ▼
Yes. In addition to capturing your moments, we enhance and retouch every picture. Our skilled team meticulously edits each image to ensure you receive stunning, flawless photos.
How are the photos delivered? ▼
Via a user-friendly online gallery where you can access, download, and easily share your photos with friends and family.
Do you do house calls? ▼
Yes. We offer house calls and on-site shoots at your preferred location. For locations outside our standard service area, an additional transport fee may apply.
How do I make payment? ▼
A 50% deposit is required to secure your booking date. The remaining balance is settled on the day of the shoot.

Delivery

  • Standard delivery: 5–7 business days (retouching & editing included, often delivered ahead of schedule)
  • Express delivery (48 hr / earlier than standard):
    • Portrait & graduation shoots: + R 500
    • Matric dance shoots: + R 700
  • Photos are delivered via an online gallery — clients can access, download, and share with friends and family.
  • All images are professionally retouched and edited.

Payment & Booking

  • A 50% non-refundable deposit is required upfront to secure the booking date.
  • The remaining balance is settled on the day of the shoot.
  • House calls and on-site shoots are available. A transport fee applies for locations outside the standard service area.

Session Policies

  • Clients must arrive 5 minutes before their session.
  • A R450 late fee applies if you arrive 10–20 minutes late.
  • If you are more than 20 minutes late, the session is cancelled — non-refundable and non-transferable.
  • Experience fees are non-refundable once booked.
  • A R700 rescheduling fee applies if you reschedule within 3 days of your session.
  • A R700 rescheduling fee also applies if the session is rescheduled 15–29 minutes after the session start time.
  • Rescheduling before session day: phone, text, or email.
  • Rescheduling on the day of the session: phone or text only.
  • Sessions may be terminated immediately for uncooperative, disruptive, or abusive conduct. The experience fee is forfeited in such cases.

Studio Rules

  1. Arrive on time. If you arrive early, wait outside — there is no waiting room or common room.
  2. Capacity limit: no more than ONE guest who is not part of the shoot.
  3. Behind-the-scenes content: do not show studio equipment (cameras, etc.) in BTS clips or videos.
  4. No food or drinks — unless being used as props for the shoot.
  5. No alcohol, drugs, or smoking on the premises (including vapes). Anyone arriving intoxicated may be asked to leave.
  6. Leave things as you find them — clean up after your shoot and dispose of all garbage in the bin.
  7. Failure to follow studio rules may result in immediate booking termination and a permanent ban.
